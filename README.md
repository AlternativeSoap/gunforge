## ğŸ® Controls

### Movement & Combat
- **W/A/S/D** - Move (Up/Left/Down/Right)
- **Mouse Move** - Aim
- **Left Click** - Shoot
- **Shift** - Dash (Costs 25 stamina, has cooldown)
- **R** - Swap Weapon (Cycles between equipped weapons)

### Interactions
- **E** - Interact (Open chests, enter shops, use portals, activate trapdoors)
- **Mouse Click** - Click on skill tree nodes, menu buttons, shop items

### UI & Menus
- **M** - Toggle Minimap
- **ESC** - Pause Menu
- **1/2/3** - Select level-up upgrade choice
- **T** - Open Skill Tree

### Tips
- Dash is invulnerable but costs stamina - use wisely!
- Stamina regenerates when not dashing
- Watch your combo meter for bonus damage
- Different weapons have different fire rates and damage
